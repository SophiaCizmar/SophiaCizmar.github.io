Fundrasing

Our school and our students raised money to offset the cost of our trip. We had to cover the cost of transportation, food, and other expenses. However, our students and PTO worked together to raise money for the trip.

Popcorn Friday

Every Friday we hosted a hot chocolate stand and the profits were pooled into the collective fun. We would sell freshly made hot cocoa, along with different toppings to cater to everyone's preferences. It was an enjoyable way for us to come together, socialize, and raise funds for our trip.
